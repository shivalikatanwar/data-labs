<p>In this project, you will:</p>
<ol>
<li>Select the topic to analyze. The topic shoud be related to your interests - kitesurfing, stock prices, human poverty, wine consumption. Whatever touches your heart.</li>
<li>Collect your data - find it among thousands of Databases available online, collect using API or be a thief and scrape some websites. <strong>Two data sources are required!</strong>.</li>
<li>Organize your data. Clean your data within the first 2 days - you won't have any time later on.</li>
<li>Visualize your data. Remember, 1 proper plot worth of 5 tables.</li>
<li>Calculate important statistics, describe your data. Start exploring the hidden knowledge of your data!</li>
<li>Prepare a model. Forecast or classify - your choice. Model should be tested and validated. Try to analyze your data from different angles and you will find very interesting insights, believe me!</li>
